WikiProject TAFI

Welcome to Today's articles for improvement (TAFI). Each week we identify underdeveloped articles that require improvement. Our goal is to use widespread collaborative editing to improve articles to Good article, Featured article or Featured list quality over a short time frame.

Here we:

  1. Identify candidate articles;
  2. Select the order they appear on the community portal and Main Page in the Today's articles for improvement section;
  3. Coordinate and collaborate to improve articles.

All articles selected to be TAFI are submitted and approved by participating editors at the nominations page, and successful nominations are placed in the schedule. The currently selected group of articles are tagged at the top with the template {{TAFI}}. After an article is no longer the current TAFI, the template {{Former TAFI}} is added to the talk page, showing the before and after improvements.

You can help with this project by improving one of this week's selected articles. For more information on how to get involved, consult the coordination page and the talk page. You can also invite an editor to join this project using the invitation found here.
