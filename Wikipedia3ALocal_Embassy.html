Please understand that this is not an actual embassy and we cannot provide assistance with visas, passports, travel questions, or immigration issues. Please do not inquire about these issues here. Although not an actual embassy either, you may find unofficial assistance at the reference desk.
Shortcut:
  • WP:LOCEMB

This is the local embassy on the English Wikipedia. More embassies in other languages may be found at meta:Wikimedia Embassy.

Welcome to the embassy of the English-language Wikipedia! This page is for discussing Wikipedia-related multilingual coordination. If you have any announcements or questions regarding international issues or the English Wikipedia, you are invited to post them here or to the Talk page for this article.
Message the embassy
 Willkommen auf der Botschaft der englischsprachigen Wikipedia. Diese Seite dient der Koordination von mehr-/vielsprachigen Angelegenheiten auf Wikipedia.
